Medical Departments





DOCCARE has a panel of dedicated and experienced consultants in various specialities to take complete care of you. All referrals to specialists, both inside and outside the Clinic, will be coordinated by your Family Physician, and we will ensure that you get the best and most appropriate specialist for you



                        

                        




FAMILY CLINIC

Though this is an era of super-specialization and groundbreaking developments in medical technology, we believe that it is the family doctor who can best offer a holistic approach to the human body by preparing a holistic treatment plan and coordinating all referrals, and bring back the personal loving care that was traditionally associated with the family doctor.




GENERAL MEDICINE

The specialists in the Internal Medicine (General Medicine) department takes care of your overall health and assesses the health status of your internal organs and systems, thus helping to improve and maintain your general health




NEUROLOGY

Our experienced super-specialists in Neurology offer comprehensive assessment and treatment for all neurological conditions, including common problems like headache, stroke, epilepsy, cerebral palsy, numbness, diabetic neuropathy, etc. The department also runs focus-area clinics like the Headache Clinic, the Stroke Management Clinic, the Parkinson's Clinic, etc




DERMATOLOGY


​The human system most under the assault of the elements, environmental pollution and harmful microbes is perhaps the skin. Our dermatologist will help you understand how to face the daily assaults and to keep your skin healthy.




PEDIATRICS


We always want only the best for our children, especially when it comes to their health. Get the best possible medical care and guidance to help you bring up a healthy and happy child




CARDIOLOGY


​Take care of your heart with expert guidance and treatment from our experienced specialist in Cardiology. Discover the many ways one can continually ensure that our heart is healthy and functioning in the way it should.




GYNAECOLOGY


​A caring and professional gynaecologist can give confidence to a woman like nobody else can. Our gynaecological team ensures that you will always be in safe hands, helping you face and address all your concerns and worries.




DIABETOLOGY


​The unfortunately all-too-common condition that is diabetes needs careful, curated, continuing assessment, management and follow-up to help you manage this condition. Get help from our Diabetology department to ensure that you enjoy the possibility of a healthy energentic lifestyle.




ENT


​We know how irritated and unhappy we become when we get a sore throat or a nasal congestion or a ear ache. Our ENT specialist will take care of all your difficulties when these sensitive organs get affected.




PULMONOLOGY


​In these difficult times, it is imperative that we take special care of our respiratory system. Our specialist consultant in Pulmonology will ensure that you literally breathe easy when pandemics have become part of of our lives.
