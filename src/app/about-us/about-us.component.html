ABOUT US

The Hospital evolved out of the desire of a doctor and his immediate family to create a health care environment of the highest quality,
combining the best of both worlds - the professional and personal care traditionally associated with medical practice and the latest in health care technology.
Since the prime mover behind this project happened to be a dental surgeon, the Spring Garden Dental Care Centre took shape and started functioning in April 2003.
