SERVICES




DocCare Medical Clinic serves
you in many ways. An understanding of
our services could give you a better
idea of how we could help you

FAMILY DOCTOR


Ask for a Family Physician on your first visit to get personal care

and guidance for the holistic treatment of your problems

SPECIALIST CONSULTATIONS


Let your Family Physician refer you to the most experienced specialists

either at the Clinic or outside, for you to get the most appropriate treatments.

DOCTOR-ON-CALL SERVICE


Give special care to your dear ones unable to visit the clinic when our

experienced Family Physician will provide non-emergency medical

consultation and treatment at your home.

PHARMACY


All the quality medicines you may need, from reputed pharmaceutical

companies, are available at the Clinic or through

online purchase and home delivery.

EXECUTIVE HEALTH CHECKUP


Get an overall assessment of you general health status to help you adopt

valid preventive measures to keep you healthy and fit in the long run.

SURGERY


Get necessary and mandated surgery done in various specialties by our

panel of experienced surgeons under the personal care and monitoring

of your Family Physician.

PHYSIOTHERAPY & REHABILITATION


Enjoy the expert services of the bio-mechanics team comprising of

experienced orthopaedicians and physiotherapists who work together to

take care of all your physiotherapy and rehabilitation requirements

CHRONIC CONDITIONS MANAGEMENT


Avail the services of focus group clinics which target the management of

chronic diseases like diabetes, hypertension, etc through in-depth treatment

protocols involving medication, diet, exercises and nutrition.

NEUROLOGICAL CONDITIONS MANAGEMENT


Our team of super-specialists in Neurology will ensure that you get

continuous support to manage neurological conditions like headache,

stroke, epilepsy, paralysis, Parkinson's etc

ONLINE CONSULTATIONS


Consult any of our doctors from the convenience of your home if you are

unable to visit the clinic or if your doctor recommends the same.

NUTRITIONAL GUIDANCE


Get detailed nutritional guidance based on your personal health status from

our team of nutritionists who are in turn guided by the Family Physicians.

DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES


Our state-of-the-art laboratory, under the quality control of CMC, Vellore,

offers you reliable, consistent and valid diagnostic tests.

PREGNANCY AND ANTE-NATAL CARE


Experience a worry-free pregnancy when the expectant mother is guided

at every step right from conception through childbirth and after

CHILD AND ADULT VACCINATIONS


Get all the recommended vaccinations, for both children and adults, at

our vaccination centre, under the watchful care of our family physicians

MINOR PROCEDURES


A full -fledged minor operation theatre takes care of all medical procedures,

both surgical and otherwise, which do not need overnight admission.

FITNESS AND EXERCISE CENTRE


Our qualified Physical Trainer, in coordination with the physiotherapist and

orthopedician, will design the ideal fitness and exercise regimen for you
